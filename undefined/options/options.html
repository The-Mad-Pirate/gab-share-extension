About Browser Permissions

Active Tab: The "Active Tab" permission gives this extension temporary access to the currently active tab when the you invoke the extension.  Access to the tab lasts while the you are on that page, and is revoked when the you navigates away or closes the tab.

Storage: The "Storage" permission gives this extension the ability to save your personal preferences such as light/dark mode to your browser's storage. 

Context Menus: A "Context Menu" is an action listed in the small popup when you right click on a web page. This permission enables the extension to create the "Share on Gab" context menu so that you can right click (and even highlight specific text) and share with one tap.

Notifications: The "Notifications" permission gives this extension the ability to send a single notification after a new gab was posted. The sent notification provides a link to click back to your newly created gab post.
